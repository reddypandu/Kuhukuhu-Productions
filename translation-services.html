Translation Services

At Kuhukuhu Productions, we provide professional translation services in multiple languages, ensuring your message reaches the right audience without losing its meaning or cultural relevance. Our expert translators specialize in entertainment, corporate, marketing, technical, and creative content.

"Breaking language barriers with precision, clarity, and cultural sensitivity."

Whether you need document translations, subtitles & closed captions (CC), or voice-over scripts, our team ensures accuracy, timeliness, and confidentiality. With a global network of language experts, we deliver translations that preserve the tone, style, and essence of your original message.

Our Benefits

Why clients choose Kuhukuhu Productions for Translation Services:

What types of content do you translate?

We translate documents, marketing content, film scripts, subtitles, legal papers, corporate communication, websites, and more.

Do you provide subtitle and closed caption translations?

Yes, we specialize in Subtitles & Closed Captions (CC) to make your media globally accessible in multiple languages.

Are translations done by AI or humans?

All our translations are handled by professional human linguists, ensuring cultural accuracy and natural flow, unlike machine translations.
