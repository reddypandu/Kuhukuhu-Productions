Voiceover Training

Voiceover Training Programs

At Kuhukuhu Productions, we don’t just create voices—we train them. Our Voiceover Training programs are designed for beginners and professionals who want to refine their vocal craft, build confidence, and master the art of storytelling through voice. Whether your goal is commercials, audiobooks, dubbing, e-learning, or corporate narration, our training helps you develop a signature sound that resonates with audiences.

“Your voice is your instrument—train it, refine it, and let it carry stories that connect.”

Training Modules We Offer

Our programs combine practical voice exercises with real-world industry projects. We ensure you not only learn the techniques but also understand how to apply them.

  • Voice Basics — Breathing, vocal projection, clarity & pronunciation.

  • Performance Skills — Expression, modulation, emotion & character building.

  • Genres — Commercials, audiobooks, e-learning, IVR, narration & dubbing.

  • Studio Training — Mic techniques, recording etiquette, editing basics.

Our Training Approach

Practical, hands-on, and industry-driven.

  • Personalized Mentorship — Learn 1-on-1 with industry experts.

  • Practical Assignments — Recordings, script readings, dubbing practice.

  • Peer & Instructor Feedback — Continuous improvement with real critique.

  • Demo Reel Creation — Build a professional portfolio for auditions & clients.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No. Our courses are designed for beginners as well as professionals looking to upskill.

Will I get practical studio training?

Yes. All trainees get to record in a professional studio environment with industry-standard equipment.

Do you provide placement assistance?

We guide you on building your demo reels, auditions, and connecting with industry networks for opportunities.
